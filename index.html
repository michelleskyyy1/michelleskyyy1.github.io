About Me

Hello Merry Traveler! Glad you wandered into this nick of the woods. Here you will find my documented travels of the places I’ve frequented around the world. Poke around and see whether you may want to visit one of these destinations yourself. A little about me! My name is Michelle, a girl whose desire to travel is only humbled by her wallet. Travelling to me is one of those experiences you need to make the absolute most of. Whether it’s a week in Old Quebec or Kelowna, heck even a few days in San Diego, there is so much we can learn about one place even if we can only grasp a piece of it. So here I am, documenting my journeys, big or small, as I make my way, inch by inch, around the world. Follow along to see where I wind up next!
